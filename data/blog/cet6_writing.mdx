#### 题目: The Impact of the Internet on Our Daily Life

##### 引言：
In terms of **internet**, I couldn't agree with it more that internet play an essential role <u>in our growth / in our daily life</u>. With the rapid development of <u>technology / society / economy</u>, the **internet** has become an indispensable part of our lives. This phenomenon has aroused public concern.

##### 主体段：
###### 数据论证：
Firstly, the **internet** has greatly <u>facilitated negatively impacted</u> <u>communication / sustainable development / the development of society / career development / physical and mental health</u>. Based on <u>a survey conducted by CCTV, the national TV station in China / a survey conducted by Stack Overflow, a well-know media concerned with **internet** / an essay published in Wikipedia</u>, over 70% people interviewed agree that **internet** is indispensable for our lives. 

###### 举例论证：
On the other hand, it is undeniable that the internet can greatly promote our career development. Take Linus Torvalds, the creator of Linux, as an example. His work on open-source software has revolutionized the tech industry, and it would not have been possible without the internet.

On the other hand, it is undeniable that the internet can greatly promote our career development. Take Yann LeCun, a pioneering researcher in artificial intelligence, as an example. His work on convolutional neural networks (CNNs) has significantly advanced the field of AI, and it would not have been possible without the extensive resources and collaborative opportunities provided by the internet.
